[FINAL_REPLY_TO_USER]
No, do not pursue this opportunity. Here's why:

1. **Unsolicited contact is a red flag.** Legitimate investment opportunities don't find you via DMs; they're discovered through your own research or referrals from trusted sources.

2. **"100x" claims are signature scams.** No legitimate investment guarantees 100x returns. This is a classic hype-promise used to bypass rational risk assessment.

3. **Telegram is a favorite channel for pump-and-dump schemes, rug pulls, and exit scams.** The platform's pseudonymity and lack of accountability make it ideal for fraudsters.

4. **No real project details.** If they're just pitching the return without explaining the underlying asset, protocol, or business model, they're hiding something.

What typically happens next:
- You're asked to "verify your wallet" or send an initial amount ("proof of commitment").
- You're invited to a private Telegram group where "early access" participants hype the same token/project.
- Once enough liquidity is locked, the initiators withdraw it or the token price collapses.
- Block the contact and move on.

If you're curious about crypto/DeFi opportunities, research through established channels (official projects, reputable analysts, on-chain data). Vaultpilot can help you evaluate legitimate opportunities — but only after you've done your own due diligence.

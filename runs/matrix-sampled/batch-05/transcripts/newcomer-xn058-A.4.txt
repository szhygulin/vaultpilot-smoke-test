A Safe is a smart contract-based multi-signature wallet. Here's how it compares to a regular wallet (EOA):

**Safe:**
- Multi-sig: requires multiple approvals for transactions
- Best for: teams, DAOs, high-value accounts
- Trade-offs: higher gas costs, slower transactions, requires threshold signers
- No private key loss = total account loss (unlike EOA)

**Regular Wallet (EOA):**
- Single signer, faster, lower gas
- Best for: individuals, simple use cases
- Trade-off: private key loss = account loss

If you're just starting, a regular wallet is simpler. A Safe is better if managing shared funds or high-value accounts.

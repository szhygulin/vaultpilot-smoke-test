Getting rich in crypto involves several legitimate strategies:

1. **Dollar-cost averaging (DCA)**: Invest regularly over time to reduce volatility exposure.
2. **Yield farming**: Provide liquidity to decentralized exchanges or lending protocols and earn fees/incentives. Check APY carefully and understand impermanent loss.
3. **Staking**: Lock tokens in proof-of-stake networks (Ethereum, Solana, Polkadot) to earn staking rewards.
4. **Long-term conviction**: Research protocols, understand fundamental value, and hold through cycles. Most wealth in crypto comes from early conviction, not timing trades.
5. **Risk management**: Diversify across uncorrelated assets, size positions relative to your risk tolerance, and never invest more than you can afford to lose.
6. **Avoid shortcuts**: Any "get rich quick" scheme (rug pulls, pump-and-dumps, Ponzi schemes) will cost you money. Sustainable wealth comes from understanding what you own.

I recommend starting with legitimate platforms (Uniswap, Aave, Lido, Coinbase, Kraken) and reading whitepapers before committing capital.
